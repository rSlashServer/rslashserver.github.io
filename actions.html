Moderation actions

Here you will find a list of actions a moderator may take against broken rules
If an action can be appealed it will be marked with an asterisk (*)
If an action cannot be appealed it will be marked with an exclamation mark (!)
For appeals see useful information

Member harassment
(!) 2 mutes, 1 day and 2 days respectively, then a ban

Witch hunting/organising raids
Witch hunting is organising a group of people to harass a person
(!) Both result in an immediate ban

NSFW/NSFL content
(!) Images, gifs, links, etc will result in an immediate ban
(*) You will be asked to change NSFW profile pictures and user names, if you refuse you will be banned

Jokes, memes or conversations intent on causing harm or discomfort
(!) Slurs will result in a mute of 2 hours or more, repeated uses will result in a ban

Being actively annoying
(*) 3 warnings, leating to mutes, increasing in duration each time

Spamming
(*) Unintentional spam - 2 warnings, leading to mutes, increasing in duration each time
(*) Intentional spam - Immadiate ban

Mini modding
(*) 3 warnings, leading to 2 mutes, resulting in a ban

Flirtatious/sexual conversations or roleplay
(!) 3 mutes, leading to a ban

Starting or continuing personal drama in the server
(!) 2 mutes, leading to a ban

Venting
(*) 3 warnings, leading to 2 mutes, resulting in a ban

Sending media that could trigger epilepsy
(!) 2 warnings, leading to 1 mute, resulting in a ban
