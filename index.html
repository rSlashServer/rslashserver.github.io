rSlash server rules

Please ping @Moderator when needed, not individual mods

Moderators have final say over all rules
If you believe a moderator is abusing power contact Dunk


Server wide rules

All communications must be in English
We cannot moderate languages we cannot speak

All users must be at least 13 years old
This is a requirement in Discords ToS
You will be kicked, but you may rejoin the server once you reach 13

NSFW content is not tolerated
Regardless of whether it's a meme, gif, profile picture or username

Jokes, memes or conversations intent on causing harm or discomfort will not be tolerated
This includes but is not limited to racism, ableism, suicide, rape, school shootings, the holocaust

"mini-modding" is not allowed
This can include telling users to stop spamming, saying a meme isn't allowed, and telling people they aren't old enough
Ping the mods if needed, and direct them to the rules

No flirtatious/sexual conversations or roleplay
Please keep that to DMs. This also involves any sort of images, gifs or links

Keep personal drama out of this server
If you have issues with a member work it out in DMs, or block them

Venting is not tolerated
We understand you might need to let it out, but this is not the right place to do that


Text channels

No ASCII art
These are images made with text

No media that could trigger epilepsy
Such as an emote with fast flashing colors

Keep messages condensed
Don't send 10 messages in a row that are 3 words each, it's spam

Do not over use emotes in messages or reactions
It can be annoying to read

Keep channels on topic
For example, rSlash related conversations stay in the rSlash category so they're easy to find

Do not farm XP
Sending messages quickly or contributing nothing to conversations will get you the no-xp role


Voice channels

Do not mic spam
Including heavy breathing, shouting, and unwanted music
This is hard to moderate, and we cannot punish members based on claims
If a moderator is available to join the call they will
Recording is NOT recommended due to the different laws in different regions
